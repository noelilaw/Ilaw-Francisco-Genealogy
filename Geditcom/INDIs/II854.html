Personal and Family Information

Gregoria was the daughter of Joaquin Martinez and Saturnina Legaspi Martinez. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Jr Pioquinto Delos Reyes. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Silverio Martinez Delos (?-?), Sixto Martinez Delos (?-?), Alberto Martinez Delos (?-?), Bonifacio Martinez Delos (?-?) and Maximina Delos Reyes (?-?).
