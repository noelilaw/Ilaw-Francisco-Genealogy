Personal and Family Information

Crescencia was the daughter of Francisco Leyva and Maximina Delos Reyes Leyva. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Martin Tuano. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Pauline Leyva (?-?), Marcy Tuano (?-?), Godofredo Leyva (?-?), Venancio Leyva (?-?) and Fernando Leyva (?-?).
