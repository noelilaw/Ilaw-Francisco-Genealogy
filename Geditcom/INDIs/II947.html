Personal and Family Information

Maddalena was born on 7 FEB 1872, the daughter of Giacomo Rutigliano and Benedetta Marrone. The place is not known.

She died on 14 FEB 1959. The place is not known.

Her husband was Matteo Squicciarini. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Michele (1901-1954).
