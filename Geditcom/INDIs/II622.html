Personal and Family Information

Michele was born on 20 APR 1901, the son of Matteo Squicciarini and Maddalena Rutigliano. The place is not known.

He died on 29 NOV 1954. The place is not known.

His wife was Apollonia Cianciotta, who he married on 23 FEB 1930. The place has not been found. Their three known children were Magdalena Squicciarini (1930-), Matteo (1932-2001) and Michele (1933-2003).
