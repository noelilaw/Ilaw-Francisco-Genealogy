Personal and Family Information

Appollonia was the daughter of Giuseppe Soranno and Anna Cianciotta. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Giuseppe Lanzillotta. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Laura ().
