Personal and Family Information

Giacomo was the son of Giuseppe Rutigliano and Madalena Cimini. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Benedetta Marrone. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Maddalena (1872-1959).
