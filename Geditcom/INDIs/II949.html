Personal and Family Information

Margiuseppa was the daughter of Oronzo Soranno and Maria Carbonara. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Michele Squicciarini. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Matteo (1866-1950).
