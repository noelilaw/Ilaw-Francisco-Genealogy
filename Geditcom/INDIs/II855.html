Personal and Family Information

Silverio was the son of Jr Pioquinto Delos Reyes and Gregoria Martinez Delos Reyes. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Pia Nicolas Delos Reyes. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their only known child was Susana Delos Reyes (?-?).
