Personal and Family Information

Valentina was the daughter of unknown parents. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Alberto Martinez Delos Reyes. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Luciano Cruz Delos (), Epifanio Cruz Delos (), Regina Delos Reyes (), Hermenegilda Delos Reyes () and Raymundo Cruz Delos ().
