Personal and Family Information

Carolina was the daughter of unknown parents. The date and place of her birth have not been found.

Her husband was Honorato Delos Reyes Oliveros. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Astraea Aquino (), Marcosa Aquino (), Anoly Aquino (), Thelemacus Aquino () and Hestia Aquino ().
