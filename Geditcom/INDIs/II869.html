Personal and Family Information

Martin was the son of unknown parents. The date and place of his birth have not been found.

His wife was Crescencia Leyva Tuano. They were married, but the date and place have not been found. Their five known children were Pauline Leyva (), Marcy Tuano (), Godofredo Leyva (), Venancio Leyva () and Fernando Leyva ().
